Meet Eve,
She's electric.

Eve is based on a 2021 Citroen E-dispatch with a range of 150 miles. You can charge her from 0%-80% in 40 minutes.

She's kitted out with a pop-up roof, 5 seats, two double beds, a fridge, a toilet, and an induction hob powered by solar panels.
