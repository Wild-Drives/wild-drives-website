Meet
Eve

Eve is based on a 2021 Citreon E-Dispatch.

{% Image "app/assets/img/wild-drives-5.jpg", "A group of people enjoying the van and a picnic", "image-rounded rotate-143" %}

Everything you could need, including the kitchen sink

Low stress electric
adventures are here

We'll cover everything to take the pop-out out of long-range journeys.

  • We provide charging network membership cards so you spend less time fiddling with logins
  • We'll help you plan your route and recommend the best spots to stop and charge

Included as standard

75KwH battery Your milage will vary, but we expect at least 150 miles per charge
100KW charging Charge 0-80% in 40 mins
5 Seats Three in the cab, two in the back
Two Double Beds One in the pop-up roof, one below
Pop Up Roof A scenic roof that lets in the breeze and the view
Sink Running water with a 20 litre tank, washing up liquid, scourer.
Fridge Tall enough for a bottle of wine
Induction Hob Cook without gas, powered solar energy. Pans, kettle, cutlery, and chopping board included.
USB-A Plugs (Five!) Charge all your devices off-grid
Heater Our only gas appliance, useful for those chilly nights

Extra add-ons

Shower A simple shower-in-a-bag, heated by the sun
Toilet A portable toilet with privacy tent
Bedding & Towels Made from comfy bamboo
Folding Table & Chairs Useful for picnics
Pizza Oven & Ingredients Ooni pizza oven, gas included
Driveaway awning More storage space for your stuff
Toaster A low-watt travel toaster you can run off the leisure battery
European travel pack Travel safely and legally in Europe
