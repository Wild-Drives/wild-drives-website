We're one of the first all-electric campervan hire companies in the world.

Our vans feature luxury and sustinable campervan interiors, with everything you need for a wonderfully comfortable trip, including a toilet, and induction hob.

All Wild Drives campervans conversions by Love Campers use sustainable materials. All wood is FSC certified. The vans include a solar panel for electricity. Natural Fibre insulation is used in the vans, using sheep's wool or hemp. And we have beautiful bamboo surfaces!
